===========================================================
FORMAT RULES - STRICT
===========================================================

1. QUESTION NUMBER: Must start with "Q1." "Q2." etc
   ✓ CORRECT: Q1. What is the capital of India?
   ✗ WRONG: 1. What is the capital of India?
   ✗ WRONG: Question 1: What is the capital of India?

2. OPTIONS: Must be A) B) C) D) on separate lines
   ✓ CORRECT:
   A) Delhi
   B) Mumbai
   C) Chennai
   D) Kolkata

   ✗ WRONG: A) Delhi B) Mumbai C) Chennai D) Kolkata
   ✗ WRONG: a. Delhi b. Mumbai c. Chennai d. Kolkata

3. ANSWER: Must be "Answer: X" on new line after options
   ✓ CORRECT: Answer: B
   ✗ WRONG: Correct Answer: B
   ✗ WRONG: Ans: B

4. SECTION: Must be in brackets [Section Name]
   ✓ CORRECT: [General Knowledge]
   ✗ WRONG: Section 1: General Knowledge

5. SPACING: Add blank line between questions
   - After question text
   - After each option
   - After answer

6. NO COMBINED LINES: Don't put question and options on same line
   - Question on its own line(s)
   - Options each on separate line

===========================================================
IMAGE HANDLING
===========================================================

To add images to questions:
1. Insert image in Word document BEFORE the question it belongs to
2. Image will be automatically linked to next question

Example:
[Insert Image Here - This image will be linked to Q1]

Q1. What is shown in this image?
A) Option A
B) Option B
C) Option C
D) Option D
